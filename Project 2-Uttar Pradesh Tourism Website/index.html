About Uttar Pradesh

Uttar Pradesh is a land where history lives in every monument, where spirituality flows through the Ganges, and where culture echoes through ancient cities like Varanasi, Agra, and Lucknow. From the majestic Taj Mahal to the sacred ghats of the Ganga, every part of UP tells a story of India's rich heritage.
